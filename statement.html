		为什么要做这个网站？因为翻译的歌词写在本子上可能会丢失，而且不方便分享。我翻译歌词的主要原因有两个。一个是《沉思录》，好像是一个罗马皇帝写的，翻译过来的句子简直了~~看完后理智-50，对译者的怒气+100。另一个是歌曲《寄明月》的英文版，这首歌听着还不错，然而英文歌词与原版中文歌词有很大差异，它为了顾全韵律，采用的是意译的方式翻译，抛弃了原有的意象，保留了情感。考虑到英文的词汇局限性，我觉得可以放弃音律（反正不是我唱【手动狗头】），保留意象与情感，毕竟一首歌有它的背景，为了音律而放弃背景的做法有些本末倒置。我翻译的歌词不考虑韵律（其实是因为菜），更关注意象与情感，希望大家喜欢。
		Why do I do this website? Because the translated lyrics may be lost in the book, and it is not convenient to share. There are two main reasons why I translate lyrics. One is "meditation", which seems to be written by a Roman emperor. The translated sentences are  ~ ~ ~ after reading, the reason is - 50, and the anger to the translator is + 100. The other is the English version of the song "Send to Bright Moon". This song sounds pretty good, but the English lyrics are very different from the original Chinese lyrics. It uses free translation for the sake of full rhyme, discarding the original imagery retains emotion. Taking into account the limitations of English vocabulary,  I think we can give up the rhythm (I'm not a singer "manual dog head") and retain the image and emotion. After all, a song has its background, so it is putting the cart before the horse to give up the background for the sake of rhythm. The lyrics I translated do not care rhythm (actually I'm a green hand), and pay more attention to image and emotion. I hope you will enjoy it.
